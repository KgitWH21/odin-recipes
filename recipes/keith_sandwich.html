Description

Keith's Preferredmethod for making a plain sandwich.

Always good for a quick meal

Ingredients

  • Any kind of bread
  • Some type of filler (I prefer peanut butter, but it can be any type of meat)
  • Any other things you want in the sandwich

Steps

  1. Get two slices of bread
  2. Insert your filler
  3. Insert anything else you want
  4. Heat the sandwich up in the microwave (Optional)
  5. Serve on plate and enjoy!
