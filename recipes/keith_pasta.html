Keith Pasta

Description

This is the pasta Keith makes at home in under 20 minutes

A one pot pasta his wife taught him how to make.

Ingredients

  • Any type of pasta
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Ground turkey (or any type of meat)
  • Half an onion
  • One bellpepper
  • Any other veggies you like!

Steps

  1. Place some olive oil in a pan
  2. Insert meat and cook, season as desired
  3. Chop up all veggies then put them in the pan
  4. Cook meat and veggies until meat is fully cooked and veggies start to get soft
  5. Pour pasta sauce into the pan
  6. Add pasta
  7. Add water proportional to the amount of pasta
  8. Shake some salt and a little bit more olive oil on the water
  9. Turn the heat up to almost max, then stir well until pasta is soft
  10. Turn the heat down to a simmer, then let it sit until wifey get home
  11. Reheat when she gets home
  12. Add cheese or whatever else you want. We like red pepper flakes.
  13. Enjoy the meal
