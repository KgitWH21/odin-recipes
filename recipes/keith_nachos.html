Keith Nachos

Return to homepage

Description

Keith's party favorite, inspired by the fiestas of is youth!

Easy, quick, and fun to make!

Ingredients

  • A bag of plain tortilla chips
  • Ground beef, turkey, or chicken
  • Taco seasoning
  • One bellpepper
  • A bag of shredded cheese
  • Any other toppings you want
  • Olive oil
  • Aluminum foil (One sheet)

Steps

  1. Pour a small amount of olive oil into pan
  2. Cook meat, seasoning with taco seasoning (don't forget to add water!)
  3. Throw chopped bellpepper and other veggies in. Cook until soft.
  4. Turn on the oven to about 375 degrees (You'll use it later)
  5. Get a cooking tray and line it with a sheet of aluminum foil
  6. Pour some chips on the tray. Make sure to cover the entire tray
  7. Take meat and veggies from pan and spread evenly over all the chips
  8. Sprinkle shredded cheese evenly over all of the chips
  9. Slide the tray of nachos into the oven and wait for about 10 to 15 minutes
  10. Remove tray from oven and enjoy!
